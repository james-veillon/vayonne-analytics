Summary

Vayonne Analytics researches and develops market timing systems based on tools called "KeyConstants".

There are several KeyConstant tools, and they are designed to be used together. When used correctly, they provide excellent leading indicators.

Once "tuned" for each market, they do not need to be changed. The settings that worked this year also worked last year, and can be expected to work in the future.

To learn more, please visit the page.

Free Webinar

This weekly group training explains the basic KeyConstant tools, applied to various stocks, commodities and Forex. This isn't a beginner's course for new traders -- it's a free opportunity to learn if these tools are a good fit for your trading style.

Guaranteed Trading Signals

This service is offered to active traders who don't want to watch the markets all day. All clients receive Buy/Sell signals with suggested Stop and Target levels in Forex markets (equity index, commodity and Treasury futures coming soon).

Private Training

This online training builds on the Free Webinar by applying the tools to the markets you trade, over a series of 5 sessions.

Custom Analysis

This service extends the Guaranteed Trading Signals service by providing signals for markets you select. Note: This service is limited to 12 clients per year.
